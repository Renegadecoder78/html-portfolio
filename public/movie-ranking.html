The Best Movies According to Timothy

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Fast & Furious

It has a lot of action pack and a good story line.

The Terminator

This one was an interesting sci-fi.

Big

I remember seeing this when it came out. The funny thing is you see Tom Hanks being a big kid.

Big Trouble in Little China

This had a of action and comedy in it. Made it very entertaining.

Ghostbusters

This one had me on the edge on my seat waiting for one of them to do something to crack me up.
